Here are some facts about Capybara

  • Belonging to the cavy family (Caviidae), their closest relatives are actually guinea pigs and rock cavies
  • Capybaras can dive and stay underwater for up to 5 minutes at a time—often falling asleep in the water whilst keeping their nose at the edge of the banks
  • are capable of reaching speeds of up to 35 kilometers an hour—that’s as fast as a horse!
